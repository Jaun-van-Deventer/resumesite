About me

Greetings! I'm Jaun Van Deventer, an aspiring full-stack/web developer driven by a lifelong passion for technology. From tinkering with computers since the age of 12 to my current journey into the world of coding, my love for the digital realm has only deepened over the years.

My story is one of self-driven exploration and a relentless pursuit of knowledge. Fueled by an innate curiosity, I took the plunge into coding this year, guided by the desire to transition from my current field to a realm where my heart truly lies. Being entirely self-taught has been both a challenge and a testament to my commitment. While formal education may not have been in the cards, my enthusiasm and quick learning pace have propelled me forward.

Currently well-versed in JavaScript and dipping my toes into Python, I am eager to expand my linguistic repertoire, with plans to incorporate additional languages in the near future. My journey also involves mastering tools such as Node.js, React, and Django, as I strive for proficiency in both front-end and back-end development.

My toolkit extends beyond programming languages to include HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Git. These skills, combined with my hands-on experience, position me as a versatile developer ready to tackle diverse challenges.

I am fueled by a dual love for learning and technology, and I am on the lookout for opportunities to transform this passion into a full-time endeavor. Join me on this exciting journey as I continue to evolve and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of web development.
