About me

Hello! I'm Jaun Van Deventer, and I'm on an exhilarating journey to become a full-stack/web developer. My passion for technology has been a guiding force since I first started working on and fixing computers at the tender age of 12. Over the years, my love for technology has only deepened, fueling my desire to pursue a career in web development.

In 2023, I made the pivotal decision to dive into coding, driven by a burning ambition to transition from my current field into one that aligns with my passions and interests. Self-taught and driven by an insatiable thirst for knowledge, I've embraced coding as a means to turn my love for technology into a fulfilling career path. Despite not having had the opportunity for formal education, my dedication and rapid learning pace have been instrumental in my progress.

Currently, my skill set includes JavaScript and C#, with a sprinkle of Python in the mix. However, I'm not one to rest on my laurels; I'm continuously expanding my linguistic repertoire and have my sights set on mastering additional programming languages in the near future. Alongside honing my programming skills, I'm delving into essential tools and frameworks such as Node.js, React, and Django, gearing up to excel in both front-end and back-end development.

In addition to programming languages and frameworks, I'm well-versed in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Git, essential tools in the developer's arsenal. Moreover, I've dipped my toes into the realm of databases, having dabbled with PostgreSQL.

I approach every coding challenge with enthusiasm and a hunger for learning, eager to grow both personally and professionally. My ultimate goal is to transition into a full-time role in web development, where I can channel my passion and expertise into creating innovative and impactful digital solutions.

Join me on this exciting journey as I continue to evolve and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of web development.
Let's build something extraordinary together!
